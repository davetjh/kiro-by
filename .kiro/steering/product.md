# Product Overview

Super Kiro World is a 2D platformer game inspired by classic side-scrolling games. Players control Kiro, who can absorb enemy powers and use them to defeat other enemies and progress through levels.

## Core Gameplay

- Side-scrolling platformer with physics-based movement
- Power absorption mechanic: suck up enemies to gain their abilities
- Four power types: None (default), Fireball, Sword, Ice Breath
- Enemy variety with different behaviors and speeds
- Collectibles for scoring and level progression
- Lives system with respawning

## Game Features

- Scrolling camera that follows the player
- Enemy respawn system
- Particle effects for visual feedback
- Platform collision detection
- Score tracking and level completion
- Approximately 2 minutes of gameplay per level

## Visual Identity

- Uses Kiro brand colors (Purple #790ECB)
- Dark theme aesthetic (#1a1a1a background)
- Kiro logo as player sprite
- Color-coded power system with visual effects
